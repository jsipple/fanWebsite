Bio

Lindsey Stirling is a violinist, dancer, and composer who creates and performs music that combines classical and electronic music.

In 2010 Lindsey Stirling appeared on America's Got Talent making it to the quarter finals. After appearing in America's Got Talent she went on to create videos, working with cinematographer Devin Graham. Creating Crystallize in 2012, which helped launch her career and now has over 200million views.
